About TAXI Antonio

Your Taxi Šibenik Service, Run Personally by Antonio

TAXI Antonio is a locally-operated taxi service based in Šibenik, run by Antonio Šakić with over a decade of experience on the roads of Dalmatia. When you call, you speak directly with me, not a call center, not a middleman, just clear and direct communication from booking to drop-off.

Around Town

Your Local Taxi in Šibenik

Short, on-demand Taxi Šibenik rides across town and the nearby coast, plus the everyday runs locals and visitors ask for most.

Hospital Pickups & Drop-offs

I drive passengers to and from Šibenik General Hospital, and I'm allowed to take the car right into the hospital grounds. That means no long walk from a distant car park, which helps a lot for appointments, discharges, and anyone travelling with elderly or less-mobile family. Just tell me if you need a hand to the entrance.

Hotel Pickups & Drop-offs

I cover all the local hotels, from Amadria Park on the Solaris resort to D-Resort and D-Marin at Mandalina marina and Bellevue near the old town. I can meet you at reception with a name sign and help load your luggage.

Bus, Train & Ferry

Catching an early bus, a train from the Šibenik or Perković station, or landing late off a catamaran? I connect you with the bus station, the train stations and the town ferry port right on time, with a hand for your bags when the schedule is tight.

Island Connections

Šibenik is the gateway to its own islands. I get you to the Jadrolinija ferry and catamaran port for Zlarin, Prvić, Kaprije and Žirje, and to the boat for Obonjan, then pick you up when you are back on the mainland.

Shopping Centers

Bringing a big shop home? I run to and from the Dalmare and Supernova shopping centers on the edge of town, and there's always room for the bags.

Marina Pickups & Drop-offs

I collect and drop off boat crews at the local marinas: D-Marin Mandalina in Šibenik, Marina Zaton and ACI Marina Skradin nearby, and Kremik and Frapa further down the coast. Plenty of room for luggage and provisions.

Quick answers about booking, pricing, and local trips around Šibenik.

Booking and Pricing

How do I book a taxi from Šibenik?

Call +385 99 447 1013, message me on WhatsApp, or send an email. I need your pickup location, destination, and preferred pickup or arrival time.

How far in advance should I book?

I take last-minute requests within my working hours, but booking in advance guarantees availability.

What does "fixed price" mean? What's included?

My fixed prices include transportation, fuel, tolls, and help with luggage. No hidden fees. Extra stops or long waits may cost more, and I'll always tell you upfront.

Do you take advance payments?

Yes. Confirming a booking requires a 20% advance payment (minimum €20). Rides priced at €20 or below require full payment in advance. You can also pay the full amount upfront if you prefer. Advance payments can be made by bank transfer (SEPA) or card, with the remaining balance settled with me on the day of the transfer.

Local Trips in Šibenik

Do you do hospital pickups in Šibenik?

Yes. I take passengers to and from Šibenik General Hospital and I'm allowed to drive into the hospital grounds, so there's no long walk from a car park. Let me know if you need a hand to the entrance.

Can you take me to the ferry for the islands?

Yes. I drop off and pick up at the Šibenik ferry and catamaran port for Zlarin, Prvić, Kaprije and Žirje, and the boat to Obonjan.

Do you pick up from Šibenik hotels, the bus and train stations?

Yes. I serve all the local hotels, the bus station, the Šibenik and Perković train stations and the ferry port, with luggage help and a name sign on request.

Do you accept card payments in the car?

Yes, I accept cards and other contactless payment methods.
