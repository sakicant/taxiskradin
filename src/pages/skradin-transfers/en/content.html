Private vs. Bus & Train

Why Book a Private Transfer Instead of the Bus or Train?

Buses and trains out of Skradin run on their own schedule, not yours, and Skradin itself has no train station at all. You would first need a connection to a bigger town, then wait for a service that stops in every place along the way, hauling luggage at both ends. A late arrival or a missed connection can cost you half a day.

A private Skradin transfer means I collect you at your exact address and drive you straight to your destination. My fixed price covers up to 4 passengers in a comfortable Škoda Superb, so for a couple or a family it is often cheaper than separate bus or train tickets, and always faster and easier.

You get direct communication with one local driver from booking to drop-off, a clean and air-conditioned car with free Wi-Fi, and a price agreed upfront with no meter running. I sell transport only, so there is no per-person pricing and nothing to sign up for beyond the ride itself.

FAQ

Skradin Transfers: Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Prices

How much is a taxi transfer from Skradin to Split?

A private taxi transfer from Skradin to Split is a fixed €140 per car for up to 4 passengers, with tolls, fuel and luggage included.

Are the transfer prices fixed?

Yes, a fixed price per car for up to 4 passengers, agreed upfront. No meter, no surprises.

How far in advance should I book?

At least 2 hours ahead so I can confirm the car and your pickup time. For a ride sooner than 2 hours, call or WhatsApp me directly and I will help if I can.

How do I pay?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.

On the Road

Is the transfer private or shared?

Every transfer is private and door to door, in a Škoda Superb. No vehicle sharing and no extra stops unless you ask for them.

Can you stop along the way?

Yes. On longer transfers I am happy to stop for a coffee, a photo or a quick sight. Just let me know when booking.

Do you also cover trips from Šibenik?

Trips that start in Šibenik are handled on my sister site, taxisibenik.hr. It is the same driver and the same car, just the right page for that starting point.

Can you accommodate a larger group?

My Škoda Superb fits up to 4 passengers. For larger groups I can arrange it through local, hand-picked partners. Just contact me for details.
