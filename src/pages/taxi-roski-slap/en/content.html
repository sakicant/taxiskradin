Your Taxi to and from Roški Slap, Krka National Park

Roški Slap is the quieter, upstream set of waterfalls in Krka National Park, reached by road in the hills above Skradin. I run a private, fixed-price taxi and transfer to and from Roški Slap in a comfortable Škoda Superb, door to door. No meter, no waiting at a rank, just one local driver you book directly.

How My Roški Slap Taxi Works

Skradin-Based, Booked Ahead for Roški Slap

I am a private taxi based in the Skradin area, not a rank cab parked at the entrance, so booking ahead is how it works. I bring you to Roški Slap and back, drop you and return at a time we agree, or carry on to Split, Zadar, the airports and beyond once you have seen the falls.

The price is fixed per car, up to 4 passengers, agreed before we set off, with luggage and tolls included. Whether you are combining Roški Slap with the Skradin boat entrance or heading straight onward, tell me your plan and I will confirm the price.
