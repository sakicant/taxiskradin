Around Town

Your Local Taxi in Skradin

Short, on-demand Taxi Skradin rides around town and along the river, plus the quick transfers visitors and locals ask for most.

Krka National Park Boat Dock

Skradin is the main entrance to Krka National Park. I drop you at the riverfront where the park boats leave for the Skradinski Buk waterfalls, and I am back to collect you when you return. Tell me your plans and I set a pickup time that fits the last boat.

ACI Marina Skradin

I collect and drop off boat crews right at ACI Marina Skradin, with plenty of room for luggage and provisions. Send your pontoon or berth number and I meet you there. For a fixed marina price, see my ACI Marina Skradin transfers.

Old Town & Riverfront

Heading to dinner at one of the konobas along the Skradin waterfront? I run you from your accommodation to your table and back again, so you never worry about parking or the walk home after a long meal.

Šibenik & the Coast

Skradin sits just up the river from Šibenik. From here I connect you to the Šibenik bus station, the ferry port and the hospital. For rides that start in Šibenik itself, see my sister service at taxisibenik.hr.

Hotels & Holiday Homes

I cover the hotels, apartments and villas in and around Skradin. I can meet you at reception or your door with a name sign and help load your luggage into the car.

Airport & Long-Distance Runs

Beyond town, I handle the longer trips too: fixed-price transfers to Split and Zadar airports and to cities across Dalmatia. See my airport transfers for the set fares.

Booking and Pricing

How do I book a taxi in Skradin?

Call +385 99 447 1013, message me on WhatsApp, or send an email. I need your pickup location, destination, and preferred pickup or arrival time.

How far in advance should I book?

I take last-minute requests within my working hours, but a booking needs at least 2 hours notice so I can confirm the car and your pickup time. Booking further ahead guarantees availability.

What does "fixed price" mean? What's included?

My fixed prices include transportation, fuel, tolls, and help with luggage. No hidden fees. Extra stops or long waits may cost more, and I'll always tell you upfront.

Do you take advance payments?

Yes. Confirming a booking requires a 20% advance payment (minimum €20). Rides priced at €20 or below require full payment in advance. You can also pay the full amount upfront if you prefer. Advance payments can be made by bank transfer (SEPA) or card, with the remaining balance settled with me on the day, in cash or by card.
