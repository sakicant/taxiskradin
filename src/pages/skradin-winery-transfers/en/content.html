Bibich & Sladić

A Short, Scenic Hop from Skradin

Both wineries sit in the hills just above Skradin, an easy ride through the Krka hinterland. I bring you to the door and back, so everyone in the group can taste and relax while I do the driving. The price is fixed per car and agreed before we set off.

Transport Only, Not a Tour

I am a taxi and transfer driver, not a tour agency. I drive you to the wineries and back at a fixed price for the car, up to 4 passengers, agreed in advance. If you would like, I wait nearby and bring you home whenever you are ready, or come back at a time we set.

Skradin Winery Transfers: Frequently Asked Questions

How much is a winery transfer from Skradin?

It is a fixed price per car, up to 4 passengers, agreed in advance. It depends on which wineries you visit and whether I wait or return later, so send me your plan on WhatsApp and I will confirm the exact price before you book.

Do you organise the tasting?

No. I handle the transport only. You book the tasting with the winery and pay them directly. I am happy to suggest who to contact, but I do not charge for the visit or per person.

Can you wait and take us back?

Yes. I wait nearby and bring you home whenever you are ready, or come back at a time we set. Just let me know when booking.

Can we visit both Bibich and Sladić?

Yes. Both are close to Skradin and easy to combine in one trip. Tell me what you would like and I will plan the timing and the fixed price.
