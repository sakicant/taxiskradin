Private vs. Shuttle

Why Book a Private Airport Transfer Instead of a Shuttle or Bus?

Public transport between the airports and Skradin is inconvenient at best. Buses from Zadar and Split airports run infrequently, stop in multiple towns, and never go door to door, so you still need another ride at the end. Shared shuttles sound convenient until your fellow passengers have different drop-offs and a 90-minute transfer turns into a three-hour ordeal with luggage.

A private airport transfer from Skradin means I pick you up at your exact address and drive you directly to the terminal, or meet you in arrivals and take you straight to your accommodation. My fixed price covers up to 4 passengers in a Škoda Superb, so the more of you there are, the better the value per person compared to any shuttle or public option.

I monitor every incoming flight in real time. If your plane is delayed, I adjust automatically and wait at no extra charge. I sell transport only, at a fixed price per car agreed in advance, so there are no per-person surprises.

FAQ

Skradin Airport Transfers: Frequently Asked Questions

Booking & Payment

How much is a taxi transfer from Skradin to Zadar Airport?

A private airport transfer from Skradin to Zadar Airport is a fixed €105 per car for up to 4 passengers, with tolls, fuel and luggage included. Split Airport is €110.

How do I find my driver at the airport?

I wait in the arrivals hall after baggage claim, holding a clear sign with your name.

How far in advance should I book?

At least 2 hours ahead so I can confirm the car and your pickup, and earlier for early-morning flights and high season. For a ride sooner than 2 hours, call or WhatsApp me directly.

How do I pay?

A 20% advance payment (minimum €20) confirms the booking, by bank transfer or card. The balance is paid to me on the day, in cash or by card.

Flights & Extras

What if my flight is delayed?

I monitor your flight in real time using the flight number you give at booking, adjust your pickup automatically, and wait at no extra charge.

Do you cover Zagreb and Dubrovnik airports?

Yes, on request. Zadar and Split are the two closest airports and my headline routes, but I also run to and from Zagreb Airport (ZAG) and Dubrovnik Airport (DBV). Just ask through my contact page for a fixed price.

Do you also cover trips from Šibenik?

Trips that start in Šibenik are handled on my sister site, taxisibenik.hr. It is the same driver and the same car, just the right page for that starting point.

Do you provide child seats?

Yes, child and booster seats are free on request. Just mention the child's age in your booking notes.
