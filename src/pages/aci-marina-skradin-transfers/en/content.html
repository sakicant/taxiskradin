FAQ

ACI Marina Skradin Transfers: Frequently Asked Questions

Where do you pick up at ACI Marina Skradin?

Right at the marina, by your berth or the marina reception, whichever suits. Tell me your pontoon or boat name when you book and I will find you.

How much is a transfer to Split Airport?

A fixed price per car for up to 4 passengers, agreed before you travel, with tolls, fuel and luggage included. See the price list above for every route.

How far in advance should I book?

At least 2 hours ahead so I can confirm the car and your pickup time, and earlier for early-morning flights and high season. For a sooner ride, call or WhatsApp me directly.
